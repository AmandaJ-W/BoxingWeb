Welcome to Lemonjelly Martial Arts

Who We Are

We are a small-town, locally-run gym with an aim to strengthen and empower people of all walks of life. We offer classes for all ages and all levels - from beginner to advanced. We boast a high teacher-to-student ratio with experienced instructors.

What We Do

We currently offer the following classes:

  • Boxing (youth/women/adult)
  • Kickboxing (youth/adult)
  • Muay Thai (all)
  • Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (all)
  • Self-defense courses
  • Personal training
